Data Deletion Instructions

According to Facebook policy, users must be able to request deletion of their data. If you have installed or used the Monopoly Deal Card Game and wish to delete your data, you may request deletion using the methods below.

Automatic Data Deletion

If the app supports account deletion directly inside the game settings, deleting your account there will automatically remove all related data stored on our servers.

Manual Data Deletion Request

You can also request data deletion manually by contacting us via email.

Send an email to:
customersupport@jbusentertainment.com

Please include the following information:

  • Your name
  • Your Facebook User ID or email used for login
  • A statement requesting deletion of your data

Your data will be deleted permanently within 72 hours of receiving your request.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

  • Facebook authentication data
  • Gameplay data (scores, progress, settings)
  • User profile information stored in Firebase

Contact

If you have questions, contact us at: customersupport@jbusentertainment.com
